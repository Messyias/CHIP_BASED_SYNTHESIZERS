TIA Synth
================
One Atari TIA chip and one Teensy 2.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Thanks for your interest in my TIA Synth.  There's a video below that covers all of the features I included on the synthesizer.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
